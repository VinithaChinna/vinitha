BENIFITS:

Saves Fuel and Time
OTP is read by the tag reader at the plaza and the toll amount is deducted automatically, when the vehicle approaches the toll plaza. The vehicle with FASTag doesn't need to stop at the toll plaza for the cash transaction

SMS alerts for transactions
Customer will receive SMS alerts on his registered mobile numbers for all the transactions done in his tag account

ONLINE RECHARGE
Customer may recharge his tag account online through, Credit Card/ Debit Card/ BHIM UPI or Net Banking

No need to carry cash
Customer doesn't need to worry about carrying cash for the toll payments

Web portal for customers
Customers can access their statements by logging on the OTP customer portal
