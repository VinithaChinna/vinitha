ABOUT OTP:

OTP is a simple to use, reloadable tag which enables automatic deduction of toll charges and lets you pass through the toll plaza without
stopping for the cash transaction.

OTP is a perfect solution for a hassle free trip on national highways. OTP is presently operational at 30 toll plazas across state highways.
More toll plazas will be brought under the OTP program in the future.

OTP is an electronic toll collection system in India, operated by the state Highway Authority of India (SHAI).

It employs Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) technology for making toll payments directly from the prepaid or
savings account linked to it.
It is affixed on the windscreen of the vehicle and enables to drive through
toll plazas without stopping for transactions.
The OTP can be purchased from official OTP
issuers or participating Banks and if it is linked to a prepaid account,
then recharging or top-up can be as per requirement.As per SHAI, OTP has unlimited validity.

7.5% cashback offers were also provided to promote the use of OTP.
Dedicated Lanes at some Toll plazas have been built for OTP.
In January 2019, state-run oil marketing companies IOC, BPCL and HPCL have signed MoUs enabling the use of
OTP to make purchases at petrol pumps.
